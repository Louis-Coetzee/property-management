APP FEATURES OVERVIEW
=====================

1. BUSINESS TOOLS
-----------------
CRM Features:
- Client Management (full CRUD, contact details, activity status)
- Lead Management (capture, source tracking, status management, assignment)
- Sales Pipeline
- Quote Management (create quotes, line items, tax, status workflow, PDF export)
- Invoice Management (create from orders/quotes, line items, tax rates, payment recording)
- Payment Tracking (multiple payment methods: cash, card, eft, bank transfer)
- Activity Tracking (calls, emails, meetings, video calls, notes, tasks)
- Team Management (roles: owner, admin, manager, supervisor, member)
- Branches (multi-location support)
- Departments (organizational structure)
- Messaging (team messaging, direct messages, group conversations)

Product & Service Catalog:
- Products (SKU, stock quantity, pricing, categories, specifications)
- Services (pricing, duration, categories)

Team & User Features:
- User Availability (weekly schedule, exclusion dates, timezone)
- Consultants Management (staff as consultants, hourly rates, availability)
